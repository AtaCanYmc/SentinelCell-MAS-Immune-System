# Architecture Decision Records (ADR) Index

This directory contains the historical record of all architectural decisions made during the design, development, and evolution of the SentinelCell MAS Immune System.

## ADR Status Lifecycle

We follow a structured architectural decision process:
- **Proposed**: The decision is under review by the development team and technical jury.
- **Accepted**: The decision has been approved and implemented.
- **Superceded**: A newer decision replaces or deprecates this decision.
- **Rejected**: The decision was reviewed and not accepted.
